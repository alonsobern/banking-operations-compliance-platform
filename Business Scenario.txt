Business Scenario

NovaBank is a fictional retail bank that wants to improve visibility into its daily operations, customer transactions, payment activity, and compliance monitoring.

The bank currently lacks a centralized analytics platform for monitoring operational KPIs, suspicious transactions, payment failures, and customer engagement trends.

The goal of this project is to simulate a real-world banking analytics solution using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

The platform will:

analyze customer transaction behavior,
monitor operational banking activity,
detect suspicious or high-risk transactions,
track payment processing performance,
support compliance and reporting workflows.

The solution is designed as an MVP but follows scalable architecture principles for future enhancements.
